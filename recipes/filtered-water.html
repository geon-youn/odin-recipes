Filtered Water

Description

Feeling thirsty? Lacking ingredients but you desire a low-calorie, highly refreshing drink? This filtered water recipe will come in clutch.

Ingredients

  • Tap water
  • Water filter of your choice (e.g. Brita)

Steps

  1. First, ensure your water filter has its filter installed.
  2. Then, use the faucet to fill your water filter's dirty tank with water.
  3. Wait for the water to become filtered.
  4. (Optional) If you desire cool water, refrigerate the water filter for 20 minutes.
  5. Pour the filtered water into a cup or waterfall.
