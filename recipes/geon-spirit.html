Geon's Spirit

Description

Geon's Spirit is an alcoholic beverage that incorporates rum, pepsi, coffee, and milk that will let you drink a lot and get you intoxicated quickly. A combination of caffeine and alcohol will make all your problems go away.

Ingredients

  • Rum
  • Pepsi (or Coke)
  • Non-fresh coffee (previous day or from the morning)
  • 2% Milk

Steps

  1. Pour in 1.5 oz of rum.
  2. Pour in 1.5 oz of Pepsi.
  3. Pour in 1.5 oz of coffee.
  4. Pour in 1 cup of milk.
  5. (Optional) Add some ice.
  6. Stir.
